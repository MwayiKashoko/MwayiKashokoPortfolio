Lorenz System: A mathematical model of a set of equations. A small change in the variables can cause a drastic change in the model.

Julia Set: Like the Mandelbrot set, it is also a self-similar fractal, but it allows for a variable change in the equation.
Sine Julia Sets: Similar to the Mandelbrot and Julia Sets, but this time it involves sine in the equation.
